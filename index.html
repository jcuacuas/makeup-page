Meet Your Makeup Artist

Hi, Im Abilene. I’m a 25-year-old makeup artist with a lifelong passion for beauty and makeup. I was always the girl in school with a full face of makeup and doing her makeup whenever I had the chance! My journey began during my junior year of high school, and although I took a break after becoming a mom to my two beautiful boys, I returned more motivated and inspired than ever.

I believe makeup should enhance who you are—not change you—and I’m honored to be a part of your special moments.

My Artistry

Based in the Hudson Valley, New York, I proudly travel to serve my clients wherever they’re celebrating. I specialize in bridal glam, soft glam, full glam, and creative makeup looks, tailoring each service to enhance your natural beauty while bringing your vision to life. With over 14 years of passion for makeup artistry, my goal is to create a personalized and enjoyable experience that leaves you feeling confident, beautiful, and truly yourself. Whether you’re preparing for your wedding day, a special event, or a photoshoot, I will ensure you love every detail of your look.
