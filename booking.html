Booking

Agreement Requirements - To proceed with your booking, you must agree to the following terms. If you do not agree, please refrain from sending a deposit.

Deposits & Payments - Deposit is non-refundable. Deposit can be transferred once with 48+ hours notice. Cash and card accepted. Remaining balance due on appointment day.

Appointment Day - I always recommend to arrive with a clean, freshly washed/shaved face. There’s no price difference if you’re wearing lash extensions, or bring your own lashes. I may request reference photos. I may request permission to take photos for media purposes. You are always free to decline—your comfort is my priority. Please arrive on time. If I’m traveling to you, please confirm the location beforehand.

Rescheduling & Cancellations - More than 48 hours: Reschedule using the same deposit. Less than 24 hours: $40 cancellation fee. No-call/no-show = future bookings declined. If I must cancel due to an emergency, your deposit will be fully refunded
